# Refine. Build. Race.
## About the Team
Visit our [Team page](https://rbrevrt.hkust.edu.hk)!
  
Red Bird Racing (EVRT) is the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology’s official Formula Student racing team. Named after HKUST’s “Red Bird Sundial”, the team is dedicated to competing in Formula Student events and engaging with the local motorsport community.  

Our team brings together a diverse group of students united by our passion for motorsport and pushing the boundaries of engineering, drawing talent from the School of Engineering, School of Science, School of Business and Management, and the Academy of Interdisciplinary Studies, embracing diversity across both undergraduate and postgraduate levels. Working closely together, we are committed to designing, building and racing a competitive electric formula car on the global stage.
